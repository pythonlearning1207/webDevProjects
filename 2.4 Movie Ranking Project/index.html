Best movies according to Ray

My top 3 movies of all time

The Lion King

"The Lion King" captivates me with its powerful storytelling, unforgettable characters, and timeless themes of courage, identity, and the circle of life, creating an emotional connection that transcends generations.

I, Robot

"I, Robot" intrigues me with its thought-provoking exploration of artificial intelligence, blending thrilling action sequences with a compelling narrative that raises ethical questions about the relationship between humans and robots.

Titanic

"Titanic" enchants me with its epic romance set against the backdrop of a tragic historical event, combining breathtaking visuals, a haunting musical score, and compelling performances to create a cinematic experience that lingers in the heart long after the credits roll.
